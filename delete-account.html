Delete Your Account

To delete your account and all associated data from The Activity Hub, please follow the instructions below.

Send an email to our support team:

Email: activityhubsercive@gmail.com

Subject: Delete Account Request

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your account email address
  • Your name (as it appears in the app)
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All of your data, including:
  • Profile information
  • Activity preferences
  • Chat messages
  • Forum posts
  • Scheduled events
will be permanently deleted.

Processing Time

We will process your account deletion request within 7 business days of receiving your email.

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us at activityhubsercive@gmail.com.
