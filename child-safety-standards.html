Child Safety Standards

Last updated: December 17, 2025

The Activity Hub is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. This document outlines our standards, policies, and procedures for maintaining a safe environment for all users.

Zero Tolerance Policy

The Activity Hub has a zero tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child sexual exploitation, or any content or behavior that endangers minors. Any violation of this policy will result in:

  • Immediate account suspension or termination
  • Permanent ban from the platform
  • Reporting to appropriate law enforcement authorities
  • Cooperation with legal investigations

Age Restrictions

Our Service is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will immediately:

  1. Suspend or terminate the account
  2. Delete all associated data in compliance with applicable laws
  3. Report to appropriate authorities if required by law

Reporting Mechanisms

In-App Reporting

Users can report child safety concerns directly within The Activity Hub application:

  1. Navigate to the user's profile
  2. Select "Report User" option
  3. Choose "Child Safety Concern" as the report category
  4. Provide details about the concern
  5. Submit the report

External Reporting

If you encounter content or behavior that may involve child sexual abuse material or exploitation, please report it immediately:

  • Email: activityhubsercive@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "URGENT: Child Safety Report"
  • Include: Screenshots, user information, and detailed description

Response and Investigation

All child safety reports are treated with the highest priority:

  • Review Timeline: Reports are reviewed within 24 hours of submission
  • Immediate Action: Accounts suspected of CSAM or child exploitation are immediately suspended pending investigation
  • Investigation: All reports are thoroughly investigated by our safety team
  • Removal: Confirmed violations result in immediate and permanent removal of content and user access

Compliance with Laws

The Activity Hub complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • United States: Federal laws including the PROTECT Act, FOSTA-SESTA, and state-specific child protection laws
  • International: We comply with applicable international laws and regulations regarding child protection
  • Reporting Requirements: We report suspected child sexual abuse material to:
    • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) in the United States
    • Appropriate regional and national law enforcement authorities
    • Relevant child protection agencies

Content Moderation

We employ multiple layers of content moderation:

  1. Automated Systems: Technology-based detection of potentially harmful content
  2. User Reporting: Community-driven reporting system
  3. Manual Review: Human review of all reported content and accounts
  4. Proactive Monitoring: Regular monitoring of platform activity

User Education and Safety Guidelines

We provide safety guidelines to all users:

  • Meet in public places for first-time meetups
  • Report any inappropriate behavior immediately
  • Trust your instincts and prioritize your safety
  • Be respectful and kind to all members
  • Never share personal information with strangers

Data Retention and Deletion

In compliance with child safety laws:

  • We retain data necessary for legal investigations and reporting
  • Data related to child safety violations is preserved for law enforcement purposes
  • We comply with all legal requirements for data retention and deletion
  • Users can request account deletion, subject to legal obligations

Cooperation with Authorities

The Activity Hub fully cooperates with:

  • Law enforcement investigations
  • Child protection agencies
  • Legal proceedings related to child safety
  • International child protection organizations

We provide all necessary information and evidence to assist in investigations while respecting user privacy and legal requirements.

Contact for Child Safety Concerns

For immediate child safety concerns:

  • Email: activityhubsercive@gmail.com
  • Subject: "URGENT: Child Safety Report"
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours

For general safety questions or policy inquiries, please contact us through the app's support features.

Updates to This Policy

We may update our Child Safety Standards from time to time to reflect changes in laws, regulations, or our practices. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Updates to this document
  • Email notifications for major policy changes

Commitment

The Activity Hub is committed to maintaining a safe platform for all users. We continuously work to improve our safety measures, reporting mechanisms, and response procedures. Protecting children is our highest priority, and we will take all necessary actions to prevent and respond to child safety violations.
